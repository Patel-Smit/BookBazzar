Transcendence: My Spiritual Experiences with Pramukh Swamiji

- A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

In Transcendence, Dr Kalam, with his co-author and pupil Arun Tiwari, maps a journey of self-realization reflected in the compassionate eyes of Pramukh Swamiji, painting a delightfully open-hearted portrait of the fusion of spirituality, science and leadership.
